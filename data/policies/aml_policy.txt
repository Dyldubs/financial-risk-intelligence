ANTI-MONEY LAUNDERING (AML) POLICY
Version 3.2 | Effective Date: 1 January 2025

1. PURPOSE
This policy establishes the framework for detecting, reporting, and preventing money laundering activities in accordance with the Anti-Money Laundering and Counter-Terrorism Financing Act 2006 (AML/CTF Act) and AUSTRAC reporting obligations.

2. RISK-BASED APPROACH
All customer transactions are subject to a risk-based assessment. Transactions are scored on a scale of LOW, MEDIUM, HIGH, and CRITICAL based on the following risk factors:
- Transaction amount relative to customer's historical average
- Transaction velocity (frequency within 1-hour and 24-hour windows)
- Jurisdiction risk (sanctioned or high-risk countries per FATF list)
- Customer risk profile (account age, KYC status, prior alerts)
- Merchant category risk (gambling, cash-intensive businesses, crypto exchanges)

3. TRANSACTION MONITORING THRESHOLDS
The following thresholds trigger mandatory review:
- Single transaction exceeding $10,000 AUD: automatic STR consideration
- Structuring patterns: multiple transactions just below $10,000 within 24 hours
- Amount-to-average ratio exceeding 5x the customer's 30-day average
- More than 5 transactions per hour from a single account

4. HIGH-RISK JURISDICTIONS
Transactions involving counterparties in the following categories require enhanced due diligence:
- FATF High-Risk Jurisdictions (currently: Iran, Myanmar, North Korea, Russia-sanctioned entities)
- AUSTRAC-identified high-risk countries
- Jurisdictions with inadequate AML/CTF regimes

5. REPORTING OBLIGATIONS
Suspicious Matter Reports (SMRs) must be submitted to AUSTRAC within 24 hours of forming a suspicion. Threshold Transaction Reports (TTRs) must be submitted within 10 business days for cash transactions over $10,000.

6. ESCALATION PROCEDURE
CRITICAL tier: Immediate escalation to Financial Crime unit. Transaction hold applied automatically.
HIGH tier: Same-day analyst review required. Transaction proceeds with monitoring flag.
MEDIUM tier: Added to daily review queue. No transaction hold.
LOW tier: Logged for audit trail only.
